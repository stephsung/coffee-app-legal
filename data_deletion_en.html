Data Deletion

Last updated: Please fill in date

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data that we hold. Below we explain how to submit a request and how we will handle it.

1. In-App Deletion

  • You can delete individual records in the App (for example, coffee beans, brew records, and similar entries).
  • If the App offers "Delete account" or "Clear all data", you can use those features directly in the App.

2. Requesting Deletion From Us

If you want us to delete all of your data from our systems, please contact us using the details provided under "About Us" in the App (for example, by email), and state that you wish to delete your account or related data. We will process your request within a reasonable time and notify you when it is complete.

3. What We Will Delete

Upon your request, we will delete records and data associated with your account or device, including but not limited to: coffee bean and brew records, photos you uploaded, and preference settings. Backups or data we must retain under applicable law will be handled in accordance with legal requirements.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about data deletion, please contact us through "About Us" in the App or by any other contact method you have for us.
